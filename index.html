About Me

My name is Nate and I do one type of development (raising money). I also do the other type of development (making computer things).

I believe that clear and open communication is best. Whether I am working with colleagues or clients I prioritize being clear and concise—when we’re all in agreement, everyone wins.

The things I build focus on their essence. Whether a website, application, or program I believe it works best when it does what it’s supposed to in a simple, uncluttered way.
